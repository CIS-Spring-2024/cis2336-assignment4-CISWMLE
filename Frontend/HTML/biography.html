Biography


After going on a long culinary journey, visiting many restaurants and learning from the best,
William Le is ready to be the proud owner of the UH Sugarland Eatery.

From an early age, William loved working with food in his family's restaurant.
After honing his skills with food and data, he went off on his own looking to run an eatery.

In his spare time, William loves to spend his time with friends and family.
Outside of that, he is enriching himself with the current cultural landscape and digital media.

The main goal of the UH Sugarland Eatery is to provide both healthy and delicious food within a close, walking distance from the classes
as well as giving students an area to socialize before and after classes.
